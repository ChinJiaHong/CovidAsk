COVIDASK in Malaysia

COVIDASK is an AI chatbot developed for COVID-19 information dissemination and symptoms assessment.

Training AI with Accurate Information

We harvested the COVID-19 information from various credible sources, which include WHO (World Health Organization), CDC (Centers for Disease Control and Prevention) and Malaysia government websites.

How does CovidAsk works?

CovidAsk was developed by using Dialogflow agent . Dialogflow agent has the capability to handle conversations with you in a human-like way. Hence, you can ask any COVID-19 related question in your own way.

You may check your symptoms anytime, anywhere. CovidAsk will ask your age, gender and a series of screening questions. At the end of the screening process, CovidAsk will triage you into a risk category and provide you with a relevant guidance.

How does CovidAsk tackles the pandemic in Malaysia?

CovidAsk provides information that you need

CovidAsk gathers a myriad of information related to COVID-19. Thus, you can ask CovidAsk any question using text.

CovidAsk allows you to perform self-triage

Self-triage feature helps you understand your risk to COVID-19. Hence, you may check your symptoms whenever you feel unwell.

CovidAsk provides guidance for stress management

Sometimes, you feel stress due to a variety of reasons. You may express your feelings to CovidAsk in order to get some stress management guidance.

CovidAsk speaks multiple languages

We understand different people prefer different languages. Hence, we train CovidAsk to speak English and Malay languages.
