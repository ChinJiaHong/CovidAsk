A better way to get COVID-19 information

Training AI with accurate information

We harvested the COVID-19 information from various credible sources such as WHO (World Health Organization), CDC (Centers for Disease Control and Prevention) and official Malaysia government websites.

CovidAsk offers instant and accurate COVID-19 information

We all had problems with getting instant and reliable COVID-19 information: misinformation is being spread across the Internet and COVID-19 hotlines are overwhelmed. We are taking action to change this by feeding accurate information to AI.

More recently, in May this year, a two-minute and 48-second audio clip surfaced, containing a claim by an unidentified person who warned that Covid-19 vaccines were poisonous and had led to the deaths of 40 nurses who took their shots.

How does CovidAsk works?

COVIDASK helps people protect themselves from COVID-19, prevent its spread, and understand the facts related to the disease. COVIDASK was developed using Dialogflow agent, a virtual agent that has the capability to handle conversations with users in a human-like way.

You may also check your risk to COVID-19. CovidAsk will ask you a series of screening questions. At the end of the screening process, CovidAsk will triage you into a risk category and provide you with a relevant guidance.
